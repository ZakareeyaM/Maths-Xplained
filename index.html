// what we cover

Five grades.
One clear path forward.

Every grade has a defined set of topics in the CAPS curriculum. We work through all of them — systematically, with depth, ensuring no gap is left behind before moving forward.

Grade 08
The Foundation Year
  • Whole numbers, integers & rational numbers
  • Exponents and roots
  • Patterns, functions & relationships
  • Algebraic expressions & equations
  • Geometry of straight lines & triangles
  • Geometric figures & constructions
  • Perimeter, area & volume
  • Introduction to the Theorem of Pythagoras
  • Statistics & probability fundamentals
Grade 09
Building Algebra
  • Numbers, operations & relationships
  • Algebraic expressions — products & factorisation
  • Algebraic equations & inequalities
  • Linear functions & graphs
  • Euclidean geometry of 2D shapes
  • Theorem of Pythagoras (application)
  • Trigonometry (first exposure)
  • Surface area & volume of 3D objects
  • Data handling & probability
Grade 10
FET Phase Begins
  • Algebraic expressions, equations & inequalities
  • Number patterns, sequences & series
  • Functions: parabola, hyperbola, exponential
  • Finance, growth & decay
  • Trigonometry — definitions, special angles, graphs
  • Analytical geometry (coordinate geometry)
  • Euclidean geometry — proofs & circle theorems
  • Measurement: surface area & volume
  • Statistics & probability
Grade 11
Depth & Rigour
  • Exponents & surds
  • Quadratic, simultaneous & literal equations
  • Number patterns
  • Advanced functions & transformations
  • Finance: compound interest, annuities & loans
  • Trigonometry — reduction, compound & double angles
  • Euclidean geometry — circle theorems (proofs)
  • Analytical geometry — lines & circles
  • Statistics: regression & correlation
  • Counting principles & probability
Grade 12
Matric — The Final Push
  • Patterns, sequences & series (arithmetic & geometric)
  • Functions — all types, inverses & logarithms
  • Finance, growth & decay (advanced)
  • Calculus — limits, differentiation & applications
  • Analytical geometry — conic sections
  • Trigonometry — problem solving & proofs
  • Euclidean geometry — full circle theorem proofs
  • Statistics — regression & probability
  • Counting principles (permutations & combinations)
  • Past paper exam strategy & structured revision

// curriculum & approach

What the curriculum demands —
and how we deliver it.

CAPS · DBE Aligned

What the curriculum requires

  • ✓Full CAPS coverage for Grades 8–12, as prescribed by the DBE
  • ✓Alignment with formal assessment — class tests, assignments, and NSC exams
  • ✓Two papers per grade at FET level: Paper 1 and Paper 2
  • ✓All four cognitive levels: knowledge, routine, complex, and problem solving
  • ✓Formal and informal assessment tasks as prescribed per school term
Teaching Philosophy

What gets taught here

  • ✓Deep conceptual understanding built before procedural shortcuts are introduced
  • ✓Every topic explained with worked examples, visual reasoning, and step-by-step logic
  • ✓Real exam question practice — structured exactly as the DBE formats them
  • ✓Common error identification: we break bad habits before they cost marks
  • ✓Calculator skills, neat working, and correct method presentation
  • ✓Structured revision and consolidation before every major test or exam
